Vietnam

Vietnam War for the United states started in 1965 and ended in 1973. It was a war of land, air and sea with a total of 58,220 casualties. Vietnam was a differant type of war than the US was used to such as gorilla war tactics. Unlike in most bunkers in WWII you never knew where the enemy was hiding.


Loyal Rongholt

Loyal Rongholt joined US Marine Corp in 1965 and after boot camp and ITR(Infantry Training Battallion) he joined Echo 2/3 and was sent to Okanowa, Japan and The Phillapians for Raider Battalion training He hit Vietnam on January 2, 1966 on Operation Double Eagle which was the Northern portion of Operations Masher that the US Army was running. From there on he was in multiple engagements and operations these are some of the operations/hills he was on:

Double Eagle 1 & 2 Hastings Alaganie(near the DMZ)
Prarie 1, 2 & 3 Hills 51, 22, 41, 65
